Clarkii Outdoors

Clarkii Outdoors is a clean, private fishing log built to help anglers keep track of time on the water without adding clutter to the experience.

Clarkii remembers what you forget.

Made for real fishing days

Keep the details that matter close at hand, from trips and catches to spots, gear, notes, and photos, in a layout that stays clear and easy to revisit later.

What Clarkii Helps Track

Built for anglers who want a simple record of where they fished, what they used, and what happened.

  • Trips
  • Fishing spots
  • Catches
  • Gear
  • Notes
  • Photos

About the Name

Clarkii comes from Oncorhynchus clarkii, the cutthroat trout. The name reflects the app’s roots in thoughtful, outdoors-focused fishing and a clean, dependable approach to keeping your log.
