Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get help?

Send a message to support@clarkiioutdoors.com with a short description of the problem and any relevant screenshots or details.

Does Clarkii require an account?

No separate account is required to use Clarkii as an offline-first fishing log.

Is my data stored online?

Clarkii is designed to keep your log local first. Your fishing records are not dependent on a required online account to use the app.

How do I back up my data?

Use the app’s backup or export options if they are available in your version, and consider including your iPhone in your normal device backup routine.

What permissions might the app request?

Depending on how you use Clarkii, the app may request access to location, photos, photo saving, or motion data for features you choose to use.
