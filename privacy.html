1. Overview

Clarkii Outdoors is a fishing log app designed to help users keep track of trips, catches, fishing spots, notes, gear, and photos.

The app is built with an offline-first approach. That means the information you enter is generally intended to be stored locally on your device so you can use the app without relying on a separate online account.

2. Information You Provide

When you use Clarkii Outdoors, you may choose to enter fishing log information such as trip details, catch details, saved locations, notes, gear information, and photos.

This information is provided by you so the app can function as your personal fishing log. You control what you add to the app.

3. Device Permissions

Clarkii Outdoors may request access to certain device features only when needed to support app functionality and only if you grant permission.

  • Location access may be used so you can save fishing spots or attach location details to trips and catches.
  • Camera access may be used so you can capture photos to include in your fishing log.
  • Photo library access may be used so you can attach existing photos or save images created by the app.

If you do not grant a permission, related features may be limited or unavailable, but you can still use other parts of the app.

4. How Data Is Stored

User-entered fishing log information is stored locally on your device as part of the app’s offline-first design.

If the app offers export, backup, or restore tools, those tools are intended to help you manage your own data. Any copies you create through those tools are under your control.

5. Data Sharing

Clarkii Outdoors is not intended to sell your personal data.

The app is also not designed to unnecessarily share your fishing log information with others. Your data is meant to remain under your control unless you choose to export, share, or otherwise disclose it yourself.

6. Third-Party Services

Clarkii Outdoors may rely on standard platform services provided by Apple or the iPhone operating system to support normal app behavior, installation, and permission handling.

If additional third-party services are added in the future, this policy may be updated to describe them.

7. Data Retention

Because the app is designed to store your fishing log locally on your device, your information is generally retained until you edit it, delete it, remove the app, or remove device backups that may contain app data.

If you export or back up your data, retention of those copies depends on how you choose to manage them.

8. Your Choices

You can choose what information to enter into Clarkii Outdoors and which permissions to allow.

You can also manage permissions through your iPhone settings, delete entries in the app, or remove the app from your device if you no longer want to use it.

9. Children’s Privacy

Clarkii Outdoors is not specifically directed to children. If you believe personal information has been provided in a way that raises a privacy concern, please use the contact information below.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the app changes or as the website information is expanded.

When updates are made, the Effective Date at the top of this page can be revised to reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, you can contact: support@clarkiioutdoors.com
